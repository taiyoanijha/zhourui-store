We believe clothing
should be effortless.

Zhiurui was founded in 2016 with a single belief: that great style shouldn't require compromise — on quality, comfort, or the planet.

2026

A small team with
a big idea.

What started as a passion project in a Phnom Penh studio has grown into a globally loved brand — but our values haven't changed. We still obsess over every seam, every fabric, every fit.

We work directly with certified mills in Portugal and Japan, ensuring fair wages, low waste, and the highest quality materials available. No shortcuts, ever.

Zhourui is a certified B-Corp. Every purchase plants one tree through our partnership with One Tree Planted.

What drives us

Our values

🌿

Sustainability first

All fabrics are GOTS certified organic or recycled. Our packaging is 100% compostable. We offset every shipment.

🤝

Fair practices

Every factory partner is audited annually. We publish our supply chain in full and pay a living wage at every tier.

✂️

Crafted to last

We design for longevity, not trends. Our lifetime repair program means if it tears, we'll fix it — for free.
